Special Events!

Join the community and get involved with our wide variety of special events. Check the descriptions as many events offer separate ticketing - although a Whole Shebang pass will get you to any and every special event.

Special Events are the heart and soul of our community. From a killer pool party, to time with our fabulous legends - the Special Events provide unique ways to connect and experience the Burlesque scene in all it's glittery glory.
