Classes

The Great Burlesque Exposition offers many wonderful opportunities to take your performance skills to the next level, whether you're just starting out or a seasoned professional.

Drop-In Classes are taught by friendly and knowledgeable instructors from The Boston Academy of Burlesque Education. B.A.B.E. is New England's leading school of burlesque dance. The drop-in classes are 50 minutes long, taught Saturday and Sunday afternoons, and are included with an Exhibit Hall pass.

The Conference is the heart and soul of The Great Burlesque Exposition. Two full days of in-depth training and discussion with some of the best instructors in the world. The conference runs from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on Saturday, March 30th, and Sunday, March 31st. Register for one day or both!
