Volunteering

Our convention is the result of the work of many people, all pitching in. We welcome new people; there's no experience necessary. (After all, many of us had never run a convention before we started.)

Whether it's an hour or two at the con or an hour or two a month leading up to the con, we welcome the help. You'll be working with a great group of people.

Send us a note to >help out by volunteering!
