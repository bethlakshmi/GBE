Advertising

Can't attend The Great Burlesque Exposition? Can attend and want to make your presence have more punch? Advertise in our Event Guide!

The Event Guide is distributed to every attendee over the course of the weekend. It includes a directory to all of our vendors, listing of all performers, and a guide to all of the special events. Performers and fans will hold onto it as a keepsake, attendees will use it as a reference throughout the year, looking up the name of their favorite shop or performer.

One thousand copies of the event guide are being printed, and they will be distributed starting Friday afternoon. The Event Guide features a full-color front and back cover, and 8-pages of information on the interior. If you have questions about advertising, please drop us a line.

Artwork and payment must be received by Friday, March 15.

  • Premium Placement is one of three locations: inside front cover; inside back cover; or back cover. We determine which of the locations. Ads in a premium location may be color or black & white
  • Interior ads are on one of the interior pages of the booklet. They must be black & white or grayscale.
  • All ads must be 300 dpi JPG and correctly sized. If your ad is a lower resolution, it will not look good. If your ad is not the correct size, there may be an art charge for us to edit it.
